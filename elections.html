Biden/Sanders Unity Plan

The goal for this 110 page plan is to push Biden and the Democratic party to commit to more specific progressive goals.

  • Criminal Justice

    - Breaking the School-to-prison pipeline and replace it with a school to opportuniity pipeline through federal guidance.
    - Demilitarizing the police by limiting the sale and transfer of military weapons to law enforcement
    - Creating a federal standard for police use of foce and a national database of misconduct

  • Climate Change

    - Setting a goal of reaching net-zero greenhouse gas emissions nationally for all new buildings by 2030
    - Installing 500 million solar panels in the next five years
    - Eliminiating carbon pollution from power plants by 2035 via new technology

  • Healthcare

    - Lowering the age to enroll in medicare form 65 to 60 and giving those people greater choice in their insurance
    - A public option plan through medicare offered to all americans, charged based on income or free for low-income americans
    -The medicare program will directly negotiate costs of prescription drugs

  • Immigration


    - Ending the Trump Adminstration's discriminatory travel and immigration bans
    - Extending access to Obamacare to Dreamers
    - Increasing the number of refugees allowed into the US from Trump's 18,000 to 125,000 per year

  • Education

    - Calls for public universities to be free for families earning under $125,000 per year. and community colleges to be free for all
    - Supporting legislation to end family separation

  • Economy

    - Encouraging more publicaly-owned internet networks
    - Large investments in infrastructure, including a soon-to-be-announced green infrastructure plan
    - Expanding public and private caregiving jobs in healthcare, child care, and elder care.
